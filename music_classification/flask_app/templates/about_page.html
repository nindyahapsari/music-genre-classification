At the moment, the network can only identify 4 music genres, which are pop, rock, hip-hop and classical. These features will be extended in the future.

The audio that you want to classify must be 30 seconds long and in a wav format. You can upload the 30 seconds audio sample and choose how many music track do you want in the spotify playlist (min 1 song track). The danceability is you tempo and you can decide either you want a slow chill music or energetic fast music üéµ

After the result is given, the application will suggest spotify music playlist that include music that have the same genre.

You can copy or follow your music playlist to your own spotify account.
